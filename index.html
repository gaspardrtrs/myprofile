Gaspard des Rotours

18 years old, student at IESEG Lille. Into graffiti, football and rap.

Graffiti

I started doing graffiti when I was about 13 and I've been at it ever since. Before that I was already drawing all the time, in class, at home, on random pieces of paper. But when I picked up a spray can for the first time it was different. I go out pretty often to paint, sometimes with friends sometimes on my own. I just like being outside with music on and a wall in front of me.

I've always loved art and drawing but graffiti is what stuck the most. There's something about making something big that you can actually see from far away. Plus you get to be creative with the letters and the colors and all that.

Football

I've been playing football in a club since I was really young. I was captain of my team for a while which was cool because you have to motivate everyone and keep the energy up even when the game is going bad. Now that I'm in Lille I don't play in a club anymore but I still play with friends whenever we can.

Music

I listen to a lot of rap. My dad was really into it and he would always play it at home so I grew up with it. I don't play any instruments or anything, I just listen to music basically all the time. When I'm painting, walking, doing homework. It's always on. Rap is definitely my favorite but I listen to other stuff too sometimes.
